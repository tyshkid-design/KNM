About KNM Law Associates LLP

Who We Are

A Full-Service Law Firm Built on Integrity & Excellence

KNM Law Associates LLP is a medium-sized, full-service law firm strategically located along Kiambu Road, Nairobi. We offer high-quality, professional, and solution-driven legal services to individuals, businesses, financial institutions, and public entities across Kenya.

Founded on the principles of integrity, professionalism, excellence, and client-focused service, the firm has steadily grown into a trusted legal partner for both local and international clients seeking reliable legal representation in Kenya.

Our practice is rooted in a deep understanding of Kenyan law, regional commercial realities, and global best practices. We combine legal expertise with practical business insight to deliver solutions that are timely, effective, and commercially sound.
