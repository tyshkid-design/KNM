Our Practice Areas

Practice Area 01

Employment & Labour Law

We advise employers, employees, trade unions, and public institutions on the full spectrum of employment and labour matters in Kenya. Our team brings depth of experience in employment disputes, HR compliance, and workplace policy design.

We provide representation before the Employment and Labour Relations Court and guide clients through the complexities of Kenya's Employment Act, Labour Relations Act, and related legislation.

  • Employment contracts and service agreements
  • Redundancy and retrenchment advisory
  • Disciplinary proceedings and termination disputes
  • Employment and Labour Relations Court representation
  • HR policy development and compliance audits
  • Trade union negotiations and collective bargaining
  • Work permits and immigration advisory

Practice Area 02

Competition Law

Kenya's Competition Act regulates mergers, market dominance, and anti-competitive practices. Our team advises businesses on navigating the Competition Authority of Kenya (CAK) regulatory framework — from pre-merger notifications to investigations.

We act for both local and international companies operating in regulated Kenyan markets, providing strategic guidance that balances commercial objectives with regulatory compliance.

  • Merger notifications and approvals at the CAK
  • Abuse of dominant market position advice
  • Anti-competitive agreements and cartel defence
  • Market investigations and regulatory inquiries
  • Consumer protection compliance
  • Competition risk assessments and due diligence
Speak to a Lawyer
Practice Area 03

Real Estate & Conveyancing

Our real estate practice covers the full spectrum of property transactions in Kenya, from residential purchases to large-scale commercial developments. We handle every step — from initial due diligence and title searches through to registration and handover.

We have acted for major banks conducting due diligence, preparing and registering transfers, charges, mortgages, and chattel transfers across Nairobi and Kiambu County.

  • Property purchases, sales, and transfers
  • Title searches and due diligence
  • Charges, mortgages, and chattel transfers
  • Lease agreements and tenancy matters
  • Land subdivision and development agreements
  • Sectional property and apartment transactions
  • Stamp duty and registration processes
Speak to a Lawyer
Practice Area 04

Intellectual Property Law

Protecting your intellectual assets is critical in today's knowledge economy. Our IP team advises individuals, SMEs, and corporations on registering and enforcing intellectual property rights in Kenya and internationally.

We guide clients through the Kenya Industrial Property Institute (KIPI) and Kenya Copyright Board (KECOBO) processes, and advise on cross-border IP strategies.

  • Trademark registration and protection
  • Patent filing and prosecution
  • Copyright registration and enforcement
  • Trade secret and confidentiality agreements
  • IP licensing and commercialisation
  • Domain name disputes
  • IP due diligence for M&A transactions

Litigation & Dispute Resolution

Led by our litigation partner Christine Kawira Ungu, our dispute resolution practice combines courtroom experience with a deep commitment to achieving practical outcomes for clients. We maintain a strong presence in Kiambu and Nairobi courts.

We also represent clients in alternative dispute resolution (ADR), including arbitration and mediation, which can deliver faster and more cost-effective outcomes.

  • Civil and commercial litigation
  • Constitutional and judicial review matters
  • Debt recovery and enforcement proceedings
  • Arbitration and mediation representation
  • Appeals before the Court of Appeal and Supreme Court
  • Injunctions and urgent court applications
  • Land and property dispute resolution

Commercial Law

We have extensive experience working with banks, insurance companies, and corporates on complex transactional matters requiring swift, commercially astute advice.

  • Commercial contract drafting and review
  • Banking and financial services advisory
  • Insurance transactions and claims

Practice Area 07

Family & Succession Law

Family legal matters require sensitivity, discretion, and expertise. Our team handles family law proceedings with empathy while ensuring our clients' interests are fully protected throughout the process.

We guide individuals and families through some of life's most significant legal transitions — from drawing up a will to administering an estate or navigating a divorce — with professionalism and care.

  • Divorce and judicial separation
  • Child custody and access arrangements
  • Matrimonial property division
  • Wills drafting and amendment
  • Grant of probate and letters of administration
  • Estate administration and distribution
  • Prenuptial and cohabitation agreements

Corporate Law & Company Secretarial Practice

Our corporate and company secretarial team — qualified Certified Public Secretaries — provides comprehensive governance, compliance, and corporate administration services to companies, NGOs, and public entities across Kenya.

We handle everything from company incorporation and restructuring through to ongoing board secretarial support and statutory compliance, helping your organisation run with governance confidence.

  • Company registration and incorporation
  • Business name and entity structuring
  • Board and AGM secretarial services
  • Governance audits and compliance reviews
  • Corporate restructuring and mergers
  • Share transfers and capital changes
  • Annual returns and statutory filings
