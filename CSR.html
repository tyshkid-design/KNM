Program 01

J.N & Co Advocates Scholarship Fund

We believe that financial hardship should never stand between a brilliant young Kenyan and a quality education. The J.N. Scholarship Fund was established to bridge that gap — offering deserving students from underprivileged backgrounds the opportunity to pursue tertiary education, with a special focus on law, business, and social sciences.

Each year, J.N & Co Advocates awards scholarships that cover tuition, study materials, and mentorship support — ensuring recipients don't just enrol, but thrive.

📚

Full & Partial Tuition Bursaries

Scholarships awarded on a full or partial basis depending on financial need and academic merit.

👩‍⚖️

Focus on Law & Related Disciplines

Priority given to students pursuing law, governance, business, and social sciences at Kenyan universities.

🧑‍💼

Ongoing Mentorship & Support

Scholars are paired with advocates from the firm who provide academic and career guidance throughout their studies.

Apply for a Scholarship
45+ Scholars Supported

Eligibility Criteria

The J.N. Scholarship Fund is open to Kenyan citizens who meet the following criteria:

🇰🇪

Kenyan Citizen

Open to all Kenyan citizens with a valid national ID or birth certificate.

🏫

Tertiary Enrolment

Admitted to or currently enrolled at a recognised Kenyan university or college.

📊

Academic Merit

Demonstrated academic performance — minimum B plain (KCSE) or equivalent.

💰

Financial Need

From a household that cannot fully finance tertiary education independently.

Program 02

J.N & Co Advocates Football Academy

Football is Kenya's most universal language. The J.N & Co Advocates Football Academy was born from our belief that sport builds character, discipline, teamwork, and resilience — qualities that make great lawyers and great citizens alike.

We recruit talented young players from Kiambu County and surrounding areas, providing professional coaching, kit, nutritional guidance, and a structured pathway to competitive football at regional and national level.

🧑‍🏫

Professional Coaching Staff

Certified CAF coaches lead structured training sessions three times a week throughout the calendar year.

🏆

Competitive League Participation

Our teams compete in Kiambu County leagues, inter-firm tournaments, and regional cup competitions.

📖

Academics First Policy

All academy players must maintain school attendance and satisfactory academic performance as a non-negotiable requirement.

Program 04

J.N & Co Advocates Mentorship Programs

The law can be a transformative career — but navigating its pathways without guidance can feel overwhelming, especially for young people from disadvantaged backgrounds. Our mentorship programme connects aspiring legal professionals, law students, and young graduates with experienced advocates and business leaders from within the J.N & Co Advocates network.

Mentees receive structured one-on-one sessions, career planning support, practical skills development, and access to our professional network — laying a strong foundation for a successful career in law or governance.

The Mentorship Journey

1

Application & Matching

Applicants complete a short profile. Our team matches each mentee with the most suitable mentor based on career goals and practice area interest.

2

Orientation & Goal Setting

Mentor and mentee meet to build rapport, align expectations, and set clear, measurable goals for the programme duration.

3

Monthly Structured Sessions

Monthly one-on-one sessions covering career development, practical legal skills, professional conduct, and industry insight.

4

Network Events & Exposure

Mentees are invited to J.N & Co Advocates legal events, court observation sessions, and professional development workshops.

5

Graduation & Alumni Network

Successful mentees graduate into our alumni community, continuing to receive support and opportunities from the J.N & Co Advocates network.

Legal Career Mentorship

🏢

💡

Program 05

Legal Internship Programme

Our internship programme is one of the most practical ways we invest in Kenya's next generation of lawyers. J.N & Co Advocates welcomes law students, pupillage candidates, and company secretarial students for structured, hands-on internships that provide real exposure to legal practice.

Interns at J.N & Co Advocates work directly alongside our advocates — attending court, drafting documents, conducting research, sitting in on client consultations (where permitted), and participating in firm meetings. This is not a filing-and-photocopying placement; it is genuine legal experience under qualified supervision.

What You'll Gain

🏛

Court Attendance

Observe real proceedings in Kiambu and Nairobi courts alongside our advocates.

📝

Legal Drafting Practice

Draft pleadings, contracts, correspondence, and research memoranda under supervision.

🔍

Legal Research

Conduct in-depth legal research across our eight practice areas using Kenyan and comparative law.

🤝

Client & Office Experience

Observe client meetings, file management, billing, and the operations of a professional law firm.

📜

Internship Certificate

All interns receive an official J.N Advocates internship completion certificate on exit.

🌐

Professional Network

Build a real network of advocates, mentors, and peers in the Nairobi legal community.

How to Apply

The Application Process

1

Submit Application

Send your CV, cover letter, and a copy of your university transcript or law school enrolment letter to info@jnnadvocates.co.ke

2

Shortlisting

Our team reviews applications and shortlisted candidates are contacted within 10 working days for an interview.

3

Interview

A brief interview (in-person or virtual) with one of our partners to assess fit, motivation, and scheduling availability.

4

Offer & Induction

Successful candidates receive an offer letter and attend a firm induction before commencing their placement.
